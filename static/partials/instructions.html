MovieExplorer

Welcome to MovieExplorer!

MovieExplorer is a tool that allows you to explore and navigate the cinematic universe. Use it to find a movie you're in the mood for right now, to browse movies that match a particular interest, or to explore new genres and tastes!

MovieExplorer was originally developed for by Taavi Taijala as part of a research study on MovieLens. A paper describing the tool and studying usage and perceptions was published at the Symposium on Applied Computing in 2018. The tool is archived here for public use, and the source code is available on GitHub. Read the paper to learn more about MovieExplorer.

Instructions

  1. The MovieExplorer feature works in a series of rounds, each containing 10 suggested movies.
  2. Provide feedback on some, all, or none of the movies. It's okay to leave some blank, especially if you are unfamiliar with them—the system will ignore these.
  3. Click the “submit” button to get a new round of movies which take your feedback into account.
  4. Repeat this process as many times as you'd like. There's no definite stopping point, so just stop whenever you feel like it!

Tips & Tricks

  • Want to find out more about a movie? Click on the movie title to get more information about the film, including a plot description and movie trailer.
  • Want to see more movies similar to the current round? Try using the “show more movies” or “refresh round” button.
  • If you get off track, click one of the “go back to this round” buttons to go back to a previous point in the process—or click the “start over” button to begin again from scratch.
